Abstract

 
        Artificial intelligence and robots have been tools that are important in the engineering and manufacturing industry
        as it makes the processes easier for example in the car manufacturing industry. These two ideas combined exmplores the topic of
        artificial intelligence in surgical robots. This article explores the foundations of surgical robots and AI a well as the 
        advantages such as the accuracy, perception and automation and the disadvantages such as reliability, sensor noise, system integration
        and complexity as well as the future of AI and robots in the medical field.

Introduction

  Recently surgical robot system (SRS) have been receiving widespread attention, as surgeons working
  on patents requires years of expertise and practice, therefore using an SRS would increase prescient,
  accuracy and would not be limited to physical limitations such as fatigue which a traditional surgeon would experience.
